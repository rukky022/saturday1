Cautions while traveling in Tokyo

Packed Subway cars can be dangerous for any tourist. Women are more at risk. It's not uncommon for women to be touched in inappropriate places.

To avoid this, there are women only subway cars.

Bars: Roppongi is a popular nightclub in Tokyo. Many tourist get ripped off by the bar bill.

"Some people reported that at "The First Bar" in Roppongi they were drugged by the female bar hostess make them almost pass out

and then their credit card was used to charge thousands of dollars of the most expensive champagne.""

Look for ATM's that accept all international debt cards.

Drive on the left side of the road.

Illegal to use cell phones on subways

Illegal to take photo and video of police

Carry your passport at all times. Police can stop you to check for it at any time

Severe criminal penalties for possession of recreational drugs including marijuana
